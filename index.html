Dianping Education Survey

I designed an online questionnaire for the new product to help understand who the users are and how they make course-purchasing decisions by using basic AIDA model. With 583 responses, I managed to develop four clusters to inform later product design, advocate the result to departments, and put the result into practical activities. This research was considered benchmarks for follow-up studies and recognized beneficial for future product planning by product department.
